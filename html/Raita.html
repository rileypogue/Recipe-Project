Basic Raita Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 cup cucumbers
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 to 2 green chiles
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder

Instructions:

Preperation:

  1. Chill the yogurt.
  2. Rinse and chop the onion and green chiles
  3. Rinse and peel the cucumber
  4. Rinse the coriander leaves. drain and chop them finely
  5. Optional: roast 1 tsp cumin seeds, crush and place in jar

How to make:

  1. Add curd and salt to mixing bowl, whisk until smooth.
  2. Add chopped cucumbers, onions, cumin powder, coriander leaves and green chiles.
  3. Stir gently. Taste test and add more salt if needed.
  4. Garnish with cumin powder. Serve with biryani, pulao, kebabs, or paratha.
